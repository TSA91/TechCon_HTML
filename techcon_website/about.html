History of TechCon

TechCon began in 1995 as a small gathering of innovative technology enthusiasts in Silicon Valley. What started as an intimate conference with just 50 attendees has now grown into a global event attracting over 5,000 tech professionals from around the world.

Throughout its history, TechCon has been a pivotal platform for groundbreaking technological discussions, serving as a launchpad for numerous tech innovations and startup collaborations.

Our Mission

TechCon is dedicated to fostering innovation, collaboration, and knowledge exchange in the rapidly evolving world of technology. Our core principles include:

  • Promoting cutting-edge technological research and development
  • Creating networking opportunities for global tech professionals
  • Inspiring the next generation of technological innovators
  • Addressing the ethical and social implications of emerging technologies
